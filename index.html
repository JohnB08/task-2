A small trip in The Woods!

Autumn creeps ever closer.

It's time to seek out nature's plunder.

Neither plant nor beast

Something else entirely.

Yet as autumn comes, it feeds me reliably.

The self-growing gold

As the forest shares in colored splendor.

It calls to summer, to finally end her.

Yet glad I am

That summer has passed.

I look upon this yield, 'cause this too won't last.
