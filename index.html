A small trip in The Woods!

Autumn creeps ever closer.

It's time to seek out nature's plunder.

Neither plant nor beast

Something else entirely.

As autumn comes, it feeds me reliably.

The self-growing gold

The forest shares in colored splendor.

Calling to summer, to finally end her.

Yet glad I am

That summer has passed.

I take autumns yield, 'cause this too won't last.
